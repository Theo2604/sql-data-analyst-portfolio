# Sales & Revenue Analysis (PostgreSQL)

## Objective
Analyze sales data to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

## Tools
- PostgreSQL
- SQL (JOINs, CTEs, subqueries, window functions)

## Key Questions
- How does revenue change month over month?
- Which regions generate the most revenue?
- Who are the top customers and products?
- What is the average order value?

## Techniques Used
- INNER JOIN
- CTEs
- Subqueries
- Window functions (LAG)
- Aggregations

## Insights
- Electronics products generate the highest revenue
- Revenue shows consistent monthly growth
- A small group of customers contributes most revenue
